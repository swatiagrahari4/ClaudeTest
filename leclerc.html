Charles Leclerc | Scuderia Ferrari 2026
Charles Leclerc enters the 2026 season as the heartbeat of Scuderia Ferrari, continuing his quest to bring the World Championship back to Maranello. Known as "Il Predestinato" by the Tifosi, the Monegasque driver remains the grid's undisputed master of one-lap pace, boasting a record-breaking collection of pole positions. As F1 moves into a new era of technical regulations, Leclerc’s ability to extract every ounce of performance from the SF-26 is more critical than ever. Partnering with Lewis Hamilton in one of the most iconic lineups in history, Charles is focused on refining his race craft to match his blistering speed, aiming to convert his qualifying dominance into a sustained title challenge. With the weight of Ferrari's expectations and his own personal mission driving him, he is ready to solidify his status as a legendary figure in the sport's most famous team.
