Fernando Alonso: Primary Driver Aston Martin Armaco F1 Team

Fernando Alonso enters the 2026 season as the grid's most experienced warrior, now embarking on a high-stakes partnership with Honda and technical guru Adrian Newey. Driving the AMR26 under his legendary number 14, the two-time World Champion is the heart of Aston Martin’s push for the summit of Formula 1. Despite a tumultuous start to the year in Melbourne—where severe vibrations from the new power unit forced a dual retirement—Alonso remains the team's most vital asset. His "lightning reflexes" were on full display during the opening lap in Australia, and his technical feedback is now pivotal as the team works frantically to optimize energy management and reliability ahead of the Japanese Grand Prix. For Alonso, 2026 represents the ultimate "last chance" to add to his 32 career victories in an era defined by total technical reset.
