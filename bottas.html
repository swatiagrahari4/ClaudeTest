Valtteri Bottas

Valtteri Bottas, Cadillac Primary Driver

Valtteri Bottas enters the 2026 season as the veteran spearhead of the Cadillac Formula 1 Team. After a year away from the starting grid serving as the Mercedes reserve, Bottas has been hand-picked to lead the sport's newest entry into the regulation reset. Reunited with his signature number 77, Valtteri brings over a decade of race-winning experience to the Indiana-based squad. As the team debuts the MAC-26 chassis, powered by Ferrari units until the full GM powertrain arrives in 2029, Bottas' technical feedback is considered the most vital asset for Team Principal Graeme Lowdon. Known for his qualifying speed and calm professionalism, Valtteri aims to build Cadillac's foundation from the ground up, looking to repeat the early-season scoring heroics that defined his previous career chapters.
