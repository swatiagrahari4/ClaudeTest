Oscar Piastri | McLaren Mastercard F1 Team 2026
Oscar Piastri enters the 2026 season as one of Formula 1's most formidable competitors, having pushed teammate Lando Norris to the absolute limit in a championship fight that captivated the world in 2025. Now a multi-time Grand Prix winner, the Australian driver has shed his rookie status to become a cornerstone of McLaren's future. As the team defends its Constructors' Championship, Piastri's calm demeanor and exceptional tire management will be vital in mastering the complex energy deployment of the new 2026 power units. With the MCL40, Oscar looks to turn his "Ice Man" reputation into a maiden World Title, starting his campaign with massive support at his home race in Melbourne.
