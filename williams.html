Williams Racing

Williams Racing enters 2026 with a renewed sense of purpose, continuing their long-standing partnership with Mercedes for power units while significantly upgrading their internal infrastructure. The arrival of Carlos Sainz marks a major statement of intent, creating one of the strongest midfield pairings alongside Alex Albon. Under the transformative leadership of James Vowles, the team is focused on maximizing the FW48 chassis through the new aerodynamic regulations, aiming to break back into the top five of the Constructors' Championship. With a focus on sustainable growth and technical precision, the Grove squad looks to reclaim its legacy as a dominant force in the sport.
