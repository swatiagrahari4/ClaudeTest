Kimi Antonelli | Mercedes-AMG PETRONAS F1 Team 2026

Andrea Kimi Antonelli enters the 2026 season no longer as the "rookie experiment," but as a proven podium contender for the Silver Arrows. Following a debut year in 2025 where he collected 150 points and made history as the youngest-ever polesitter in any F1 format with his Miami Sprint pole, Antonelli has silenced the skeptics who questioned his leap from Formula 2. Driving the W17 under the number 12, the young Italian has spent the winter break working with performance psychologists to refine the consistency that occasionally wavered during his rookie campaign. With Mercedes appearing to have mastered the initial energy deployment challenges of the 2026 power units, Antonelli is poised to challenge teammate George Russell not just for internal supremacy, but for the World Championship itself.
