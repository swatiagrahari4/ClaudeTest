McLaren Mastercard Formula 1 Team

McLaren enters 2026 as a formidable championship contender and the defending champions for both the Constructors and Drivers Championships, having refined their aerodynamic philosophy to meet the challenges of the new technical era. The team continues to utilize Mercedes power units, maintain a strong technical partnership while focusing on their superior in-house chassis development. The lineup remains one of the most talented and consistent on the grid, featuring defending champion Lando Norris and Aussie hero Oscar Piastri, both of whom have grown into seasoned race winners. Under the leadership of Zak Brown and Team Principal Andrea Stella, the Papaya squad aims to bring the fight to the front of the pack with the MCL40, leveraging their state-of-the-art wind tunnel and simulation facilities.
