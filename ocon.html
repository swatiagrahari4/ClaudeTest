Esteban Ocon | Toyota Gazoo Racing Haas F1 Team 2026

Esteban Ocon enters the 2026 season as the experienced anchor for Toyota Gazoo Racing Haas. After a formative 2025 campaign where he established himself within the American squad, Ocon now faces the "biggest technical reset" of his career. Driving the VF-26 under his long-held number 31, the Frenchman's feedback has been vital as Haas integrates deeper technical support from Toyota. Known for his defensive mastery and clinical race management, Ocon has described the new 2026 power units as requiring a "complete rethink" of driving style. Partnered once again with Oliver Bearman, Ocon's role is not just to secure points, but to lead the team's development curve as they aim to become a dangerous force in the midfield during this wide-open new era of Formula 1.
