Mercedes-AMG Petronas F1 Team

Mercedes enters the 2026 era looking to reclaim its position at the pinnacle of Formula 1, leveraging a completely redesigned power unit developed for the new technical regulations and distributed to a lot of teams on the grid. The team begins a new chapter following the departure of Lewis Hamilton, centering its future around George Russell, who takes on the role of lead driver. He is joined by the highly anticipated Italian prodigy Kimi Antonelli, making one of the most exciting young pairings on the grid. Under the continued guidance of Toto Wolff and Technical Director James Allison, the Brackley squad aims to utilize the W17 chassis to dominate the ground- effect and active-aero requirements of the 2026 season.
