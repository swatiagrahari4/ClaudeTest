Alpine F1 Team

Alpine enters 2026 having transitioned from a full Renault works project to a Mercedes customer model, a move aimed at streamlining performance under the new technical regulations. The Enstone-based squad features an all-French-speaking lineup with the experienced Pierre Gasly and Argentine sensation Franco Colapinto, who secured a permanent seat after a breakout 2025. Under the leadership of Flavio Briatore and Managing Director Steve Nielsen, the team utilizes Mercedes power units and gearboxes as they attempt to climb back into the upper midfield with their car A526.
