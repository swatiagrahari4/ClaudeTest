Audi F1 Team

The 2026 season marks the dawn of a new era as Audi officially enters Formula 1 as a full works manufacturer. Integrating the state-of-the-art power unit developed in Neuburg with the chassis expertise of the Hinwil-based squad, Audi aims to challenge the established order from its very first race. The driver lineup combines the technical maturity of Nico Hülkenberg with the explosive potential of 2024 F2 Champion Gabriel Bortoleto. Under the strategic leadership of Mattia Binotto and Jonathan Wheatley, the four rings look to translate their legendary motorsport heritage into Grand Prix success, utilizing the debut Audi challenger to spearhead their charge into the 2026 regulations.
