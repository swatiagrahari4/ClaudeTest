Sergio "Checo" Perez | Cadillac Formula 1 Team 2026

Sergio Perez makes a triumphant return to the Formula 1 grid for the 2026 season, joining the ambitious Cadillac project alongside Valtteri Bottas. After a brief hiatus from racing at the end of 2024, "Checo" was identified by the Indiana-based team as the perfect candidate to represent the "Team of the Americas." Carrying his iconic number 11, Perez's role is foundational; he is tasked with helping Cadillac navigate their debut year using Ferrari-supplied power units while the team builds toward its own General Motors engine for 2029. With six Grand Prix wins and a reputation as a master of street circuits and strategic tire management, Perez's presence provides the stability Cadillac needs to establish itself in the pinnacle of motorsport. As the 2026 season begins, the Mexican veteran is hungry to prove that his race-winning instincts are as sharp as ever.
