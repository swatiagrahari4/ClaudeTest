Cadillac Formula 1 Team

Cadillac makes its historic entrance into Formula 1 for the 2026 season, marking the arrival of a true American works powerhouse. The team's first-ever chassis, the MAC-26, is named in honor of racing legend Mario Andretti, reflecting the team's deep roots in motorsport heritage. While General Motors works on its own proprietary power unit for future seasons, the team debuts with Ferrari engines to ensure immediate competitiveness. Leading the charge is one of the grid's most experienced lineups: Valtteri Bottas and Sergio Perez. Under the leadership of Team Principal Graeme Lowdon and CEO Dan Towriss, Cadillac aims to bridge the gap between American innovation and the pinnacle of global engineering.
