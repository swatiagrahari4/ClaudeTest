Red Bull Racing

Red Bull Racing enters the 2026 season marking a historic milestone as they debut the first-ever Red Bull Ford powertrain. This transition away from Honda marks the team’s evolution into a fully independent manufacturer, combining Milton Keynes engineering with the resources of Ford. The driver lineup remains anchored by four-time champion Max Verstappen, while the promising new Frenchman Isack Hadjar steps into the second seat full-time to solidify the team's future. Under the strategic oversight of Laurent Mekies, the squad looks to maintain its championship dominance with the RB22 chassis despite the massive shift in engine regulations.
