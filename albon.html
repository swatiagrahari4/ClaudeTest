Alex Albon | Atlassian Williams F1 Team 2026

Alex Albon enters the 2026 season as a pillar of stability for the Atlassian Williams F1 Team. Having signed a long-term commitment that takes him through this new era of technical regulations, Albon has become the definitive face of the Grove-based team's revival. Racing with his iconic number 23, Alex is poised to reach a historic milestone this year: surpassing Nigel Mansell as the driver with the most race starts in Williams history. While the new FW48 chassis faced challenges in pre-season testing, Albon’s technical precision and ability to extract performance from difficult machinery remain the team's greatest assets. Partnered with Carlos Sainz in what is arguably the strongest midfield lineup on the grid, Albon is ready to prove that his "championship-level" consistency can lead Williams back to the podium in F1's bold new chapter.
