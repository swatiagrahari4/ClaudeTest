Isack Hadjar | Oracle Red Bull Racing 2026
Isack Hadjar enters the 2026 season having achieved the ultimate goal of the Red Bull Junior Team: a seat in the senior squad alongside Max Verstappen. After a sensational 2025 rookie campaign where he delivered podium finishes and fearless overtakes, the Frenchman has been entrusted to lead Red Bull Racing into a new era of technical regulations. Known for his aggressive "street fighter" racing style and rapid adaptability, Hadjar has quickly silenced skeptics who questioned his rapid promotion. As the team pioneers its first-ever in-house power unit with Ford, Isack’s role as the strategic support to Verstappen is vital, yet his raw speed suggests he is ready to challenge for wins in his own right. With the blue and red of Milton Keynes behind him, Hadjar is no longer just a prospect—he is a key player in the fight for the Constructors' Championship.
