Franco Colapinto | BWT Alpine F1 Team 2026

Franco Colapinto enters the 2026 season ready for his first full campaign at the pinnacle of motorsport. After a whirlwind 2024 debut and a developmental 2025 where he stepped into the Alpine mid-season, the Argentine star has secured his place as a permanent fixture on the grid. Driving the Mercedes-powered A526 under the number 43, Colapinto has earned praise for his "cat-like reactions" and growing technical maturity. With the massive commercial backing of Mercado Libre and the fervent support of a nation behind him, Franco is no longer just a replacement—he is a vital part of Alpine’s 2026 reset. Partnered with Pierre Gasly, he aims to translate his undeniable raw speed into consistent points finishes as Enstone begins its climb back toward the front of the field.
