Oliver Bearman

Oliver Bearman enters the 2026 season as a cornerstone of the Toyota Gazoo Racing Haas F1 Team's ambitious future. After a stellar rookie campaign in 2025, where he demonstrated maturity beyond his years and secured crucial points finishes, "Ollie" is now tasked with leading the American squad through the most significant technical regulation reset in a generation. Driving the VF-26, Bearman continues to leverage his deep ties with the Ferrari Driver Academy while establishing himself as a formidable force in his own right. With his signature number 87, he aims to build on the momentum of his debut season, utilizing his natural speed and technical curiosity to master the new high-electrical-output power units and push Haas toward the front of the midfield.
