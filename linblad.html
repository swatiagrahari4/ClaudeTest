Arvid Lindblad

Arvid Lindblad enters the 2026 season as the most anticipated rookie in years, making history as the youngest British driver to ever start a Grand Prix. After a dominant rise through the Red Bull Junior Team—culminating in a standout 2025 Formula 2 campaign—Lindblad has earned the second seat at Racing Bulls alongside Liam Lawson. Racing with the number 41, the 18-year-old Anglo-Swede represents the new "youth-first" philosophy at VCARB. Known for his clinical overtaking and maturity beyond his years, Lindblad has already impressed the team during 2026 pre-season testing, adapting quickly to the high-electrical-output power units. As he prepares for his debut in Melbourne, Arvid carries the momentum of a successful "Red Bull Moto Jam" showcase in India, ready to prove he belongs at the pinnacle of the sport.
