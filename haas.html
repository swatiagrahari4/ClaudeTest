Haas F1 Team

Haas F1 Team enters the 2026 season with a revitalized technical structure and a balanced driver lineup designed to push the team further up the midfield standings. Continuing their close technical partnership with Ferrari for power units and non-listed parts, Haas has also deepened its relationship with Toyota Gazoo Racing to enhance its manufacturing and testing capabilities. The pairing of Esteban Ocon and Oliver Bearman represents a blend of proven race-winning experience and raw, youthful talent. Under the leadership of Ayao Komatsu, the American outfit aims to utilize the VF-26 to deliver consistent points and capitalize on the unpredictability of the new regulation cycle.
