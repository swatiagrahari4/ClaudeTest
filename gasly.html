Pierre Gasly | BWT Alpine F1 Team 2026

Pierre Gasly enters the 2026 season as the experienced cornerstone of the BWT Alpine F1 Team. Now in his fourth season with the Enstone squad, Gasly leads the team into a historic technological shift as they transition from Renault to Mercedes power units. Racing with his signature number 10, the Frenchman's resilience was tested during a difficult 2025 campaign, but his faith in the "A526" project remains unshaken. Known for his tactical intelligence and clinical qualifying pace, Pierre is tasked with guiding the team back to the front of the grid under the new leadership of Executive Advisor Flavio Briatore. After securing a hard-fought point in the season opener at Melbourne, Gasly is determined to prove that Alpine’s early focus on the 2026 regulations will make them the "dark horse" of this new Formula 1 era.
