Scuderia Ferrari

Ferrari enters 2026 with arguably the most decorated driver lineup in the history of the sport, featuring Charles Leclerc and seven-time world champion Lewis Hamilton in his second year in red. Under the direction of Fred Vasseur, the Scuderia has focused its 2026 development on thermal efficiency and battery management to suit the new "X-mode" and "Z-mode" active aero regulations. As a full works team, Ferrari continues to produce its own chassis and power unit, carrying the immense pressure of ending their nearly two-decade-long championship drought.
