Nico Hülkenberg | Audi Revolut F1 Team 2026

Nico Hülkenberg enters the 2026 season as the veteran spearhead for Audi's historic entry into Formula 1. After a standout 2025 campaign with Sauber—where he finally secured his maiden F1 podium at the British Grand Prix—Nico has been rewarded with the lead seat in the Ingolstadt works project. Racing under his trusted number 27, Hülkenberg's vast experience is central to the development of the Audi R26 and its first-ever in-house power unit. Partnered with rising star Gabriel Bortoleto, "The Hulk" faces the pressure of leading a manufacturer team in a high-stakes regulation reset. While pre-season testing suggested that Audi is still finding its footing, Hülkenberg’s clinical qualifying pace and technical precision remain the team's most reliable asset as they begin their journey toward the front of the grid.
